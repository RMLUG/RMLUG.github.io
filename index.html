About Us

We’re a community of Linux enthusiasts and open-source advocates who come together to share knowledge, swap nerdy passions, and collaborate on projects big and small. Whether you’re a seasoned sysadmin, a tinkerer building your first distro, or just curious about Linux and open source software, you’ll find friendly faces and great conversations here. Our mission is to build a thriving local community that celebrates learning, creativity, and freedom through open source. Join us for hands-on workshops, casual meetups, and lively discussions that make Linux fun for everyone!
